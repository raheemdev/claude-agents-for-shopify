# CRO Review Prompt Template

Use this prompt to audit a Shopify store's conversion rate optimization. Work through each section systematically. For each item, note: current state, issue (if any), and recommended action.

---

## 1. Homepage Review

- What is the primary CTA above the fold? Is it clear and compelling?
- Does the hero communicate the value proposition within 5 seconds?
- Is there social proof visible without scrolling (reviews, press, customer count)?
- Are best-selling or recommended products featured prominently?
- Is the navigation intuitive? Can users find products in 2 clicks or fewer?
- Does the page load without layout shift during hero image/video load?

## 2. Collection Page Review

- Are filters available and relevant to the product category?
- Is the default sort order optimized (best sellers, not alphabetical)?
- Do product cards show enough info to make a decision (price, rating, variants)?
- Is there a clear visual hierarchy — does anything compete with products for attention?
- Are pagination or infinite scroll implemented without UX friction?
- Is the collection description present and useful (not just SEO filler)?

## 3. Product Page Review

- Is the Add to Cart button visible above the fold on all devices?
- Are product images high quality, zoomable, and showing the product in use?
- Is pricing clear, including compare-at prices and unit pricing where relevant?
- Are variant selectors intuitive (swatches for color, dropdowns for size)?
- Is shipping info, return policy, and availability visible on the page?
- Are reviews present and filterable? What is the average rating?
- Is there a size guide or product comparison tool where applicable?
- Are cross-sells and upsells shown (complementary products, bundles)?

## 4. Cart & Checkout Flow

- Does the cart use a drawer/slide-out or a separate page? Which serves this store better?
- Are cart upsells present? Are they relevant or distracting?
- Is the free shipping threshold displayed with a progress indicator?
- Is the checkout flow standard Shopify or customized? Any unnecessary steps?
- Are express payment options visible (Shop Pay, Apple Pay, Google Pay)?
- Is trust messaging present near the checkout button (secure checkout, guarantees)?

## 5. Mobile Experience

- Is the mobile navigation easy to use with one hand?
- Are tap targets large enough (minimum 44x44px)?
- Does the sticky Add to Cart bar appear on scroll?
- Is the mobile page speed acceptable (under 3s load)?
- Are forms optimized for mobile (proper input types, autofill)?
- Is the mobile cart/checkout flow frictionless?

## 6. Trust Signals

- Are trust badges present (SSL, payment icons, guarantees)?
- Is there a visible return/refund policy?
- Are real customer reviews displayed (not just star ratings)?
- Is the About page compelling and authentic?
- Are contact options easily accessible (chat, email, phone)?
- Is user-generated content (UGC) leveraged on product pages?

## 7. Urgency & Scarcity Elements

- Are stock level indicators used? Are they honest?
- Are sale timers present? Are they genuine or artificial?
- Is social proof used in real-time (e.g., "12 people viewing this")?
- Are urgency elements overused to the point of eroding trust?
